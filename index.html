Who We Are

A boutique built on operator instinct and trusted access.

Founded in 2023, VSv Inc. (Velocity & Synergy Ventures) is a relationship-led M&A advisory firm serving founders and strategic buyers in the BPO and CX ecosystem. We don't run auctions. We run discreet, founder-first processes that protect the narrative, the timing, and the confidentiality at every stage — and we draw on two decades of inside-the-business operating experience to ground every conversation.

01 / FOCUS

Sector-led

M&A advisory, strategic partnerships, and capital introductions across BPO, CX and adjacent tech-enabled services.

02 / GEOGRAPHY

Philippines-centric origination with strategic buyer access across APAC, North America, UK and India.

Our Services

Precision execution. Sector depth.

We support clients across the deal lifecycle — from initial positioning through closing — with the operator's lens that distinguishes useful advice from generic process management.

Sell-Side Advisory

Structured exit processes for founder-led BPO and tech-enabled services businesses. Confidential, controlled, and outcome-focused.

  • Positioning & teaser / IM development
  • Buyer mapping & targeted outreach
  • Process management & bid management
  • Negotiation & closing support

Buy-Side Origination

Proprietary deal sourcing for strategic acquirers seeking scalable delivery platforms. Founder networks. Local market access.

  • Target screening & thesis development
  • Proprietary outreach & qualification
  • Founder relationship-building
  • Diligence coordination through close

Transaction Support

Operator-led commercial diligence and synergy work. We pressure-test the deal narrative against real BPO operating realities.

  • Commercial due diligence
  • Operating model & delivery DD
  • Synergy sizing & integration planning
  • Client concentration & margin analysis

Deal Mechanics & Structuring

Practical structuring guidance grounded in what actually closes. Earn-outs, rollover equity, control vs. minority — calibrated to your goals.

  • Valuation framing & negotiation prep
  • Earn-out & rollover structuring
  • Minority vs. control trade-offs
  • LOI to definitive agreement support

How We Engage

A structured roadmap, discreet at every stage.

From first conversation to post-close transition, we run a deliberately staged process that protects the narrative and keeps momentum without sacrificing control. Typical timelines are indicative — every mandate is calibrated to the founder's goals.
